    Stay Protected

    Privacy Protection Tips

    Simple habits that drastically reduce your digital footprint.

    Review Permissions

    Regularly audit what you've granted. Go to browser Settings → Privacy → Site Permissions and revoke anything unnecessary.

    Use a VPN

    A VPN hides your real IP and location from every website you visit, making IP-based tracking much harder.

    Block Location by Default

    Keep location access denied globally. Only approve it for trusted apps like Maps when you actually need it.

    Privacy-First Browsers

    Firefox and Brave block fingerprinting and trackers by default. Far safer than Chrome for everyday browsing.

    Clear Cookies Often

    Cookies track your entire browsing session across sites. Clear them weekly or use a container-based browser.

    Read Before You Allow

    Before tapping "Allow" on any permission prompt, ask: why does this site need this? If unclear, deny it.
