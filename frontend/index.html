    Privacy Protection Tips

    Simple steps to protect your online privacy

    1

    Review Permissions Regularly

    Check what permissions you've granted to websites and revoke unnecessary access in your browser settings.

    2

    Use VPN Services

    A VPN masks your real IP address and location, making it harder for websites to track your physical location.

    3

    Disable Location by Default

    Keep location services turned off and only enable them for trusted websites when absolutely necessary.

    4

    Use Privacy-Focused Browsers

    Consider browsers like Firefox or Brave that offer enhanced privacy protection and tracking prevention.

    5

    Clear Cookies Regularly

    Cookies can track your browsing habits. Clear them regularly or use private/incognito browsing mode.

    6

    Read Privacy Policies

    Before granting permissions, understand how websites will use and store your personal information.

    About This Demonstration

    This tool is designed for educational purposes only to help you understand web privacy and data exposure.     WARNING: Always be mindful of what information you share online and what permissions you grant to websites.
